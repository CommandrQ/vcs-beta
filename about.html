Our Story

Vanguard Citizen Services

The Origin

Vanguard was founded by two friends with deep backgrounds in the military and technology sectors. During our service, we mastered discipline, technical precision, and the duty of care for those around us.

We realized that as digital tools became mandatory for daily life, many in our community were being overwhelmed. We brought our expertise home to ensure no neighbor is left behind.

Our Identity

  • The Guardian Standing watch against scams and security breaches targeting our community.
  • The Architect Designing digital hubs and systems that fuel local growth.

The Mission

The Radcliff and Elizabethtown corridor is where traditional life meets the digital frontier. Vanguard exists to bridge that gap. We ensure Hardin County never faces the frontier alone.
