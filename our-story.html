Our Story

Every revolution begins with a conversation. Long before the Vanguard Knights were assembled, Commander Q and Sir Lancelot crossed paths during their travels. Despite coming from different origins, they quickly discovered a shared ideology: a mutual, burning desire to change the world for the better.

What began as a shared vision soon evolved into a fierce debate on execution. They spent countless hours discussing the intricacies of how to enact real, lasting change without compromising their core values. They realized that talking was no longer enough; action was required.

Ultimately, they forged an absolute mandate. They decided to not only showcase their vision to the world, but to build a beacon—a platform designed to attract brave, brilliant individuals from different walks of life. Together, they laid the foundation for the collective that would eventually execute the Vanguard Vision.
