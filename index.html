How We Started

Vanguard Citizen Services began simply as a way to share the Vanguard Knights' journey as they rebuilt. Along the way, it grew into something much bigger—a creative studio focused entirely on lifting up our community.

Putting People First

We believe the best tools are built by listening. Before we create anything, we spend time hearing what our community actually needs. Then, we work together to build simple, helpful resources that make everyday life a little easier.

Growing Together

The Vanguard Knights are the heartbeat of everything we do. Together, our goal is to build a safe, welcoming space where technology actually works for you, not against you. We're so glad you're here.
