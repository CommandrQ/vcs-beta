My Story

← Back to Control Deck

I was born in a land completely surrounded by technology and grew up moving through all kinds of diverse communities. As a kid, I personally witnessed a lot of the heavy stuff—the violent incidents, the sickness, and the raw breakdown that still affect those exact same neighborhoods today.

Right after high school, I joined the armed forces and deployed straight into the War on Terror. I wanted to meet the world right at the spearhead of change! But when I finally came home, I saw that our communities had actually gotten worse while the real voices of reason were just getting completely drowned out.

Eventually, I started questioning the whole perspective and the mainstream narratives we are fed. I aligned with another path, looked at things differently, and jumped in to help create Vanguard Citizen Services to make a real difference where it matters!
