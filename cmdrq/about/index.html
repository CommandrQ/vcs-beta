About Me

My Story

From the time I was a kid, I have always seen the real, unvarnished truth of how our world works. That perspective followed me into my time in the armed forces, where my deployment opened my eyes to a completely different side of the world.

After my military service, I went into the tech industry, working on operations and infrastructure with major teams at Best Buy and Apple.

Today, I use all of those experiences to lead the Vanguard Knights. My mission now is to dive deep into our neighborhoods, listen to the people, and uncover the completely unique stories that make up our community.
