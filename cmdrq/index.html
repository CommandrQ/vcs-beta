The Project V Manifesto

Project V started in the most honest way possible: I sat down and asked my kids and my family what their everyday lives were really like. What they told me was painful to hear. It made me realize how many daily struggles and quiet brick walls people hit just trying to get a decent education and build a life right here in their own towns.

When the structure around us breaks down, people end up completely cut off from each other. Project V is a straight-to-the-point experiment. We are using clean, anonymous digital tools to combine the true voices of a community. By cutting out the usual political red tape, we can map out exactly what people are feeling and experiencing in real time.

The Goal: Take that data and put it directly in front of local leaders, business owners, and neighbors so we can make a clear, measurable change in how we live. We are starting with the everyday challenges of normal citizens—because a town can only move forward when its people are finally heard.
