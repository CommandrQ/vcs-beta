Community Voice Box

Project V // Hardin County

Living in Radcliff and across Hardin County, we all navigate a complex world where massive national conversations and immediate, daily local realities collide. Often, the gaps between everyday citizens and our elected decision-makers can leave communities feeling disconnected. We want to completely change that.

Our Collective Vision

Through tracking patterns and listening carefully to our neighbors, we have gathered a clear truth: our community doesn't need more political division—it needs an honest, direct path to be heard. We intentionally built this tool to be completely **anonymous**. We believe that the real, raw issue itself is infinitely more important than who said it or why they said it. When a single neighbor gathers the courage to speak up about a local friction point, they might just be acting as the exact voice that hundreds of others needed to hear.

For Citizens

Share raw friction points safely without red tape, judgment, or fear of exposure.

For Small Businesses

Highlight local infrastructural blockades directly impacting your daily commerce and staff.

For Elected Leaders

Access unvarnished, authentic real-time needs maps straight from the neighborhoods you serve.

By building this bridge, we remove the static from community progress. Whether it is clarifying a question regarding local school board transparency, identifying resource access constraints, or calling out an infrastructure bottleneck along Dixie Highway, this log turns quiet frustrations into visible data points. Together, we can help our leaders serve us with absolute clarity.

Launch Your Note Anonymously

1. Select Topic: Categorize where the community friction lies.
2. Pick Area: Identify the nearest municipal footprint inside Hardin County.
3. Map Location: Mention a street boundary, public school, or neighborhood layout.
4. Describe: Briefly explain the issue so it can be verified for the public ledger.
