Community Voice Box

Project V // Hardin County

Whether dealing with local or nationwide hurdles, it can be tough for neighbors in Radcliff and across Hardin County to get their voices heard by local leaders. We are building a bridge to fix that.

The Vision

This tool is completely **anonymous** because the issue itself is more important than who reports it. When one person steps up to share a problem, they become the voice that hundreds of our neighbors need to hear.

Citizens

Share everyday local issues safely, directly, and without any red tape.

Businesses

Point out road, utility, or school updates that impact your daily work.

Elected Leaders

See a real-time map of actual needs straight from the citizens you serve.

Submit Anonymously

1. Topic: Choose the area that fits your note best.
2. General Area: Pick the closest city footprint.
3. Location: Name a street, school, or public neighborhood.
4. Description: Briefly explain the issue so we can log it.
