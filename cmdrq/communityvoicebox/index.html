It is easy to look at the world right now and feel entirely completely overwhelmed. There are so many major problems unfolding around us that it is hard to know where to even begin. At the exact same time, people are left searching for a real sense of meaning and purpose in their day-to-day lives.

"We believe that if we can unite neighbors around a clear, real-time mission, we can start pointing our collective time, skills, and funding exactly where they are needed most—before the community is left to figure it out all alone."

The Living Guidebook

Project V serves as a simple, living map for community teamwork. It narrows the gap between everyday citizens and our elected leaders at all levels. By tracking local needs right as they happen, we take the guesswork out of progress. It gives us a path forward, reminding us that at the end of the day, whether we are an individual or a business, we are all serving the exact same community customer.

For Neighbors

Find a clear place to start. Align your unique personal strengths with real, immediate community projects and talk directly with local leaders without all the usual red tape.

For Local Partners

Cut through the bureaucracy. Invest your business resources, volunteer hours, or sponsorships directly into the transparent projects your neighbors are actively asking for.

Reflecting to Build Better

A vital part of moving forward is taking the time to look back. Project V keeps a clear historical log of the changes our community makes, allowing everyone to look back, talk honestly about what worked, and build a smarter path for the future.
