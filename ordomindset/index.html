Kentucky Crisis Resources

National Lifeline: Call or text 988 inside the US. Completely free, anonymous, and available 24/7.
Central Kentucky Line: Reach the regional Communicare Crisis Support Desk at 1-800-641-4673 for help in Hardin County footprint areas.
Crisis Text Support: Send HOME via text message to 741741 to link securely with a trained counselor.
