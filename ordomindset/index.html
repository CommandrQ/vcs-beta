Kentucky Mental Health & Crisis Support

If you or a loved one are going through an incredibly dark moment, please know you are never alone. Reach out to these free, confidential resources anytime.

National Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 inside the US. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Completely free and anonymous.

Kentucky Community Crisis Lines

Connect with the Communicare Crisis Line at 1-800-641-4673 for regional support across Central Kentucky and Hardin County footprint areas.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to link instantly with a compassionate, trained crisis counselor over secure messaging streams.
