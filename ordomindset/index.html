02. Your Practical Mental Tools

When life gets overwhelming, look at these two mental reminders to get back on track:

👁️ The All-Seeing Eye // Absolute Honesty

It's easy to make comfortable excuses when things go wrong. The Eye reminds you to sit back, breathe, and look at your problems honestly. Instead of asking why other people are breaking rules, ask yourself: "What is the absolute truth of this situation, and what can I control?"

⏳ The Hourglass of Fate // Precious Time

Your time on this earth is a beautiful, limited gift. Sand falls through the hourglass every second. Don't waste your energy frozen in worry or trapped in toxic environments. Use your moments intentionally.
